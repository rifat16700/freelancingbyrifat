1. Placing Orders & COD Rules (CRITICAL)

  • Binding Agreement: Placing any order (COD or paid) is a legally binding commitment. You confirm you will receive and pay for the product.
  • Cancellation after Dispatch: If you cancel after the parcel is shipped, or refuse delivery without valid reason, you must pay the two-way delivery charge + packaging fee as a penalty.
  • Blacklisting: Failure to pay the penalty will result in your Name, Phone, and Address being reported to national e-commerce seller blacklist groups, preventing you from ordering from other shops. Legal action (Consumer Rights Act) may also be taken.
  • Fake Orders: Placing fake or prank orders is a punishable offense. We will report you and take legal measures.
  • Order Confirmation Call: We may call you to verify your order. Please answer. If 3 attempts fail, the order may be cancelled.

2. Mandatory Unboxing Video

To claim any issue (damaged, wrong, missing item), an unboxing video is absolutely required. No exceptions.

  • Must start recording before opening the intact package.
  • Must be continuous — no cuts, no pauses, no edits.
  • Shipping label must be clearly visible at the start.
  • Without a valid video, NO claim will be accepted under any circumstances.

3. Payment Policy

  • bKash / Nagad (Manual): Send the exact amount to our number shown at checkout. Enter your Sender Number and Transaction ID (TrxID). Orders are verified manually. Using a wrong number or wrong amount — contact us immediately, otherwise the order is void.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Pay the delivery rider when you receive the product. You must pay the exact amount shown. Refusing to pay after receiving = legal action.
  • Online Gateway: You will be redirected to a secure payment page. After payment, you are returned automatically. Transaction IDs are verified — duplicate TrxIDs are blocked.
  • Advance Payment: Some orders require an advance (shown during checkout). The remainder is paid on delivery.
  • Crypto (International Only): For international customers only. Crypto is legally restricted in Bangladesh. BD users transact entirely at their own legal risk. We hold zero liability.

⚠️ Incorrect / Wrong Payments

If you send money to a wrong number or wrong amount and do not contact support immediately, the order and refund will not be valid. Always double-check before sending.

4. Refund, Return & Exchange

  • Customized Products: Zero refunds. Customized items are made specifically for you and cannot be resold.
  • Color Variation: Slight color differences due to screen/camera are normal — not a valid return reason.
  • Size Variation: Up to 0.5–1 inch variation is industry standard — not a valid return reason.
  • Wrong Size (Your Mistake): Exchange allowed but you pay both-way shipping.
  • Defective / Wrong Item: Valid claim only with unboxing video. We will replace or refund.

💰 Refund Calculation — Strict Deduction Rule

For ALL refund types: Delivery charge + Packaging cost are deducted first from your paid amount. Remaining balance is refunded via your chosen method below.

  • Option 1 – Store Voucher: Remaining amount + 5% bonus (max ৳150 bonus). Best value option.
  • Option 2 – Cash Refund: 90% of remaining balance sent to your bKash/Nagad.
  • Option 3 – 50/50: 50% cash + 50% store voucher.

  • আপনার তথ্য কোনো থার্ড পার্টিকে বিক্রি বা শেয়ার করা হয় না।
  • পেমেন্ট তথ্য সিকিউর গেটওয়ে দিয়ে যায়, আমাদের সার্ভারে সেভ হয় না।
  • অর্ডার আপডেট SMS/মেসেজে পাঠানো হতে পারে। স্প্যাম করা হয় না।

১৩. শর্তাবলি

অর্ডার করার মাধ্যমে আপনি ওপরের সব নিয়ম পড়েছেন, বুঝেছেন এবং মেনে নিয়েছেন বলে ধরে নেওয়া হবে। কোনো নিয়মে একমত না হলে অর্ডার করবেন না। পূর্ব নোটিশ ছাড়াই পলিসি পরিবর্তনের অধিকার আমাদের আছে।
